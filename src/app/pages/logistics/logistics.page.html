Use Case: Real-Time Fleet & Asset Monitoring

Challenge: Disconnected systems and legacy infrastructure limit real-time visibility into fleet movements, asset conditions, and inventory status, slowing response and increasing operational risk.

Our Solution: Our unified IoT and edge platform supports key logistics protocols and integrates with sensors, RFID, and telematics, enabling seamless connectivity across fleets, warehouses, and distribution centers.

How It Works: Connect vehicles, assets, and facilities using protocol bridges; stream real-time location, condition, and inventory data from edge to cloud via secure gateways; visualize operations with live dashboards and KPIs; and automate alerts or actions-such as rerouting or maintenance-using a low-code rule engine.
