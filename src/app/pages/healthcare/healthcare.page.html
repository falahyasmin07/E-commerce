Use Case: Real-Time Patient Monitoring & Diagnostics

Challenge: Healthcare providers face fragmented data from diverse devices and locations, making it difficult to monitor patients in real time, deliver timely care, and protect sensitive information.

Our Solution: Our edge-cloud platform connects patient monitors, imaging devices, and wearables using healthcare protocols, enabling secure, scalable connectivity for real-time analytics, AI-driven diagnostics, and proactive care.

How It Works: Deploy our solution to enable secure, autonomous data streaming and processing at the edge. Medical devices analyze and act on data locally for instant response, while essential information is synchronized with the cloud for fleet-wide monitoring, diagnostics, and compliance. Live dashboards provide operational visibility, and rule-based automation triggers alerts or actions-such as notifying clinicians or adjusting therapy-ensuring better outcomes and patient safety.
